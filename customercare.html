Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. Alternatively, use the tracking link sent to your email. For real-time updates, you can also contact our support team with your order number.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Electronics have a 15-day return window. Some items like personalized products or intimate apparel may not be eligible for return.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping: 3-7 business days
Express shipping: 1-3 business days
Same-day delivery: Available in select cities
International shipping: 7-14 business days
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient. Check our international shipping page for detailed information.
How can I change my delivery address?
You can change your delivery address within 1 hour of placing the order through your account. After that, contact our support team immediately. Once the order is shipped, address changes may not be possible and could result in delivery delays.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (available in select areas). All payments are secured with SSL encryption.
